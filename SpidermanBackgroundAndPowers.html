Spiderman


Introduction

Spiderman is your everyday neighborhood Spiderman (this is his catchphrase). He uses his power in order to protect New York from a variety of villians. This includes powerful enemies like

  1. venom.
  2. kingpin
  3. Green Goblin

Spiderman also attends Mid-Town High School, so he saves the day while managing homework, his studies, and not showing his true identity as Peter Parker to the public.
If I were in Peter Parker's shoes, most people would most likely drop out and move out, which goes to show how hard his life must be, on top of that he once had to fight his best friend because venom took over his body.

Family

Spiderman aka Peter Parker is an orphan who lives with his Aunt May and Uncle Ben after his parents Richard and Mary Parker died in a plane crash. Uncle Ben is 68 years old, and Aunt may is 52 years old while Spiderman is 28.
Mary and Richard Parkers ages are unknown but it is speculated that the ywere anywhere from their mid - late 20s through 40s. They both worked for the secret agency S.H.E.I.L.D. and it is believed they died on a mission.

Powers

Spiderman shoots spider webs out of his fingers to subdue his enemies and to travel quickly around the city to save his people. Spiderman got his powers by getting bitten by a radioctive spider. In the comics he has a wide variety of forms such as Cosmic form and Murder form.
